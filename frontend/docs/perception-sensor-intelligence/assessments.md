# Assessment Materials: Perception & Sensor Intelligence

## Chapter Assessments

### Chapter 1: Perception vs Sensing Fundamentals
1. Define the difference between sensing and perception in robotics.
2. Explain the role of perception in converting raw sensor data to meaningful information.
3. Describe how perception contributes to a robot's understanding of its environment.

### Chapter 2: Sensor Data Processing Pipelines
1. Describe the components of a typical sensor data processing pipeline.
2. Explain the purpose of preprocessing, feature extraction, and interpretation stages.
3. Identify common processing considerations like real-time constraints and noise handling.

### Chapter 3: Camera-Based Perception
1. Explain the fundamentals of camera-based perception and image processing.
2. Describe how feature detection and object recognition work conceptually.
3. Identify the challenges in camera perception like lighting variations and occlusions.

### Chapter 4: LiDAR and Depth Perception
1. Describe how LiDAR systems work and their role in depth perception.
2. Explain the process of converting LiDAR data to spatial understanding.
3. Identify the advantages and limitations of LiDAR compared to other sensors.

### Chapter 5: Sensor Fusion Fundamentals
1. Explain the concept of sensor fusion and its importance in robotics.
2. Describe the different fusion levels (signal, feature, decision, symbol).
3. Identify the benefits and challenges of combining multiple sensors.

### Chapter 6: Perception ROS 2 Integration
1. Describe how perception systems integrate with ROS 2 communication frameworks.
2. Explain the role of ROS 2 message types in perception systems.
3. Identify the communication patterns used for perception in ROS 2.

### Chapter 7: Simulation-Based Perception Testing
1. Explain the benefits of simulation-first approach for perception testing.
2. Describe how simulation environments can validate perception systems.
3. Identify the challenges of the reality gap in simulation-to-hardware transfer.

## Module Assessment

### Practical Exercises
1. Design a simple perception pipeline for a robot navigating a room with obstacles
2. Explain how you would combine camera and LiDAR data for object detection
3. Create a simulation scenario to test a perception algorithm's robustness

### Written Examination Topics
1. Compare and contrast sensing vs perception approaches in robotics
2. Analyze the trade-offs between different sensor modalities for perception
3. Design a sensor fusion strategy for a humanoid robot's navigation system
4. Evaluate the impact of the simulation-to-reality gap on perception system development
